Nuclear Power Plant

The Rooppur Nuclear Power Plant is a 2.4 GWₑ nuclear power plant in Bangladesh.
It consists of two 1200 MWe VVER-1200 reactors.
The nuclear power plant is located at Rooppur in Ishwardi Upazila on the bank of the river Padma, about 160 km northwest of Dhaka.
